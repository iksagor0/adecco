Following on from the Winter edition published in November, the year concluded with strong jobs growth and unemployment at a near historic low of 3.7%. Despite this, it was a year of uncertainty which looks set to continue into 2023. Headwinds from the cost of living crisis, the impact of increased inflation, supply chain challenges and the long-lasting impact of the pandemic continue to challenge organisations across all regions of the UK. Whilst this is a turbulent time for many industries, hiring and retaining critical talent remains a top priority. With any recession expected to be both short and shallow, businesses must continue to prioritise workforce strategy and prepare for growth in the years ahead.
