As a detective, you are responsible for investigating crime and gathering evidence to support criminal cases. You may be involved in interrogations, searches, surveillance, and interviewing witnesses. You will be expected to be able to identify patterns in criminal activity and identify potential suspects. The work of a detective involves a lot of legwork and paperwork, as well as long hours and intense pressure. You must be able to think logically and quickly when gathering evidence and working on a case. Working as a detective can be challenging, but it can also be incredibly satisfying when you are able to put together pieces of a puzzle and solve a case.
