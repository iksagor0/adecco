Following on from the Spring edition published in March, we have seen a year of significant change. As we publish the Winter edition, we now face a cost of living crisis, unprecedented energy bills, the war in Ukraine, soaring inflation and a turbulent political landscape have all contributed to a difficult economic period. With talent shortages prevailing throughout this time and unemployment at 3.5%, its lowest level for nearly 50 years, recruiting and retaining staff remains a top priority for many organisations.
