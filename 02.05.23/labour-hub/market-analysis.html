UK job market statistics and analysis

Welcome to our labour market information hub. In today’s world, it’s more important than ever for jobseekers, employers, and policymakers to stay informed about the nuances of the dynamic and ever-changing UK labour market. That’s why you’ll find a range of resources here to help you do just that – from up-to-date labour market statistics to in-depth labour market analysis and blog articles on the latest employment trends impacting the UK job market.

Central to this hub is the Adecco Labour Market Outlook, which combines regional and sectoral economic analysis from Oxford Economics, salary data from salary.com, and insight from Adecco on labour market and hiring trends. You can download your free copy of the Labour Market Outlook and use it to support your talent attraction and retention strategy.

But as the UK labour market moves so quickly, we will also use this space to bring you monthly updates on key labour market statistics – such as candidate supply and demand, unemployment rates, and redundancy rates – and put them in context of wider employment trends like talent scarcity, digital transformation, and the future of work.

Recruiting and retaining talent is a challenge for any organisation, but we hope that the labour market information hub, with up-to-date labour market statistics and in-depth labour market analysis, will help you stay informed, navigate the complexities of the UK job market, and make smarter decisions for your business and in your own career.
